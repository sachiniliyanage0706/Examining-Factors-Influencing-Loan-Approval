# 💳 Examining Factors Influencing Loan Approval
The findings aim to support data-driven decision-making for  improving loan approval processes, reducing financial risk, and promoting fair lending practices.(Analysis -MINITAB)

⭐ Academic Project | Credit Risk Analysis | Machine Learning | Financial Analytics

## 📌 Overview

This project focuses on analyzing the key factors influencing loan approval decisions using statistical analysis and predictive modeling techniques. The study examines applicant income, credit history, loan amount, employment characteristics, and demographic information to understand their impact on loan approval outcomes.

The project aims to support data-driven lending decisions and improve credit risk assessment in financial institutions.

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze factors affecting loan approval decisions
* Identify relationships between applicant financial characteristics and loan status
* Build predictive models for loan approval classification
* Evaluate the impact of credit history and income on lending decisions
* Support fair and efficient credit risk assessment

## 📊 Dataset

* Loan Approval Classification Dataset
* Source: Kaggle
* Total records used after preprocessing: 477
* Variables include:

  * Applicant Income
  * Co-applicant Income
  * Loan Amount
  * Credit Score
  * Employment Status
  * Education Level
  * Property Area
  * Previous Loan Status

## 🛠 Methodology

* Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
* Descriptive Statistical Analysis
* Correlation Analysis
* Multiple Linear Regression (MLR)
* Data Visualization
* Classification Analysis

## 📈 Key Findings

* Applicant income and credit history positively influenced loan approval
* Loan amount and credit score showed moderate positive relationships
* Financial stability played a major role in approval decisions
* Regression analysis identified key predictors for credit risk assessment
